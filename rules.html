Rule 01
5%

Daily Drawdown Limit

Your account must not lose more than 5% of the starting day balance on any single calendar day. Calculated from balance at the start of each trading day.

Rule 02
10%

Overall Max Drawdown

From your initial account balance, you must not lose more than 10% in total at any point. This is a hard stop — breaching it closes your evaluation immediately.

Rule 03
8%

Profit Target

To pass and receive your funded account, you need to grow your account by 8% from starting balance, while maintaining full drawdown compliance.

Rule 04

Violations & Breaches

Violations of drawdown rules result in immediate account deactivation. Breached accounts must restart the evaluation. Our risk engine enforces rules automatically, in real-time.

Rule 05

Consistency

Traders with extreme day-to-day P&L variance may be reviewed more carefully during verification. We fund disciplined traders, not lucky ones. Risk management quality matters.

Rule 06

Prohibited Strategies

HFT exploiting latency, account cooperation, copy-trading between our accounts, and market manipulation are strictly prohibited and result in permanent account termination.
